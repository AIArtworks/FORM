Logistics

Shipping & Returns

Every FORM study is made to order, quality-checked, and packed flat in protective board (or a rigid tube for larger sizes) so it arrives ready to frame.

Shipping

  • Free worldwide shipping on orders over $150.
  • A flat $12 applies to orders below that.
  • Production takes ~48 hours; delivery then depends on destination (typically 3–7 business days).
  • All orders ship tracked — you'll get a link by email.

Returns & replacements

If your print arrives damaged, or isn't right, contact us within 30 days and we'll arrange a free replacement or a full refund. Because prints are made to order, we can't accept change-of-mind returns on framed items, but we'll always try to make it right.

Damaged in transit?

Send a photo to our team within 48 hours of delivery and we'll reprint and reship immediately at no cost.
