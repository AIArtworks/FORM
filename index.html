The FORM method

Every bolt,
every line —
drawn.

A FORM study isn't a photograph. It's a considered drawing — every fin, spoke, and weld rendered with a draughtsman's patience, then catalogued with a study number, year, and accession code like a piece pulled from a museum archive.

Each print is produced only when you order it, on archival stock built to hold its detail for a century. No warehouse. No waste.
