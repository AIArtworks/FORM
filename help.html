How can we help?

Help Center

Answers to the questions we hear most. Still stuck? Contact us.

Are these original drawings or prints?

Every FORM study is a giclée print of an original hand-detailed drawing, reproduced on museum-grade archival paper or fine-art stock with pigment inks rated to last 100+ years.

When will my order ship?

Each print is made to order and usually leaves our studio within 48 hours. You will receive tracking as soon as it ships.

Which sizes and finishes are available?

A4, A3, A2 and A1, in Archival Matte, Fine-Art Textured, or Framed (Oak). See the Size & Paper Guide for full dimensions.

Do you ship worldwide?

Yes — we ship worldwide with tracked delivery. Shipping is free on orders over $150; a flat $12 applies otherwise.

What is your returns policy?

If a print arrives damaged or is not right, we will replace or refund it within 30 days. See Shipping & Returns.

When are the other volumes released?

Volumes I & II are live. Mechanical Anatomy, Blueprint, Workshop and Industrial Icons are in development — join the list for early access.
